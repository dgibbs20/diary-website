Getting Started

Up and writing
in minutes

1

Download & Create Your Account

Download diAry from the Google Play Store. Create your account using your email address. You'll receive a verification code to confirm your identity — this is how we keep your journal private and secure from the very first moment.

2

Set Your Mood & Security Preferences

Each time you open diAry, a mood check-in greets you. Select how you're feeling — Happy, Sad, Bored, Romantic, Naughty, Angry, Sleepy, or Sick. Then configure your biometric lock and privacy settings to your comfort level.

3

Choose Your Journal Type

Tap the + button to create a new entry. Choose between Text Journal (classic lined paper experience), Voice Journal (tap to speak), or Handwritten Journal (write or draw by hand). Each type captures your thoughts in the way that feels most natural.

4

Write Your Entry

Express yourself freely. For text, type on the beautiful lined page. For voice, tap the gold microphone and speak — your words transcribe automatically. For handwriting, select your page style and write or draw naturally using any stylus or finger.

5

Let Your AI Companion Respond

After saving your entry, tap the AI Companion button. Choose Friend mode for emotional support, Mirror mode for therapeutic reflection, Insight mode for deeper analysis, or Auto to let diAry read your energy and respond accordingly. Your companion is always listening.

6

Use Ghost Mode & Burn Mode

For sensitive entries, enable Ghost Mode to block screenshots and auto-lock the app. Toggle Burn Mode before saving to set an entry to self-destruct after a chosen time period. Your most private moments can be expressed, processed, and released — permanently.

7

Track Your Emotional Journey

Visit the Analytics tab to view your mood trends over time, writing streaks, and emotional patterns. The more you journal, the richer your personal insights become. diAry learns with you and grows alongside your journey.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 — GMX Quantum LLC

Introduction

GMX Quantum LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the diAry mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. By using diAry, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the diAry service:

  • Account Information: Email address, first name, last name, and encrypted password upon registration.
  • Journal Content: Text, voice recordings, handwriting, and any content you create within the App. This content is stored securely and is never shared with third parties.
  • Mood Data: Mood selections and emotional tracking data you provide voluntarily.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized, aggregated app interaction data used to improve performance and features.
  • Device Information: Device type and OS version for technical support purposes only.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide, operate, and maintain the diAry application; process journal entries and generate AI Companion responses; send account verification and security notifications; provide customer support when requested; improve App features and user experience; and process subscription payments through secure third-party processors.

AI Processing

The diAry AI Companion feature uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses to your journal entries. Your journal content is transmitted to Anthropic's servers solely for the purpose of generating AI responses. We never use your journal entries to train AI models.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted storage, JWT authentication with token expiration, and biometric authentication support.

Ghost Mode & Burn Mode

When entries are deleted via Burn Mode, they are permanently and irreversibly removed from our servers. We cannot recover deleted entries. Ghost Mode prevents screenshots and screen recordings within the App.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and journal entries for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support@gmxquantum.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

diAry uses Anthropic Claude API (AI responses), OpenAI Whisper (voice transcription), Amazon Web Services (cloud infrastructure), RevenueCat (subscription management), and Yahoo SMTP (email verification).

Children's Privacy

diAry is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. Contact us immediately at support@gmxquantum.com if you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact support@gmxquantum.com.
